Recipe for Cooking Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a popular West African one-pot dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, peppers, and spices. It's a flavorful and aromatic meal that's often served at social gatherings and celebrations. Jollof rice is a staple in many countries, including Ghana, Nigeria, and Senegal, with each country having its own unique twist on the recipe.

Recipe Information

  • Preparation time: 30 mins
  • Cooking time:45 mins
  • Number of servings: 4
  • Difficulty:Beginner

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2-3 tomatoes, blended
  • 1 red bell pepper, blended
  • 1-2 Scotch bonnet peppers (optional)
  • 2-3 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: protein of choice (chicken, beef, or fish)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add the blended tomatoes, red bell pepper, and Scotch bonnet peppers (if using) to the pot. Cook for about 10-15 minutes until the mixture thickens and the oil begins to separate.
  4. Add the thyme, curry powder, and salt to the tomato mixture. Stir well to combine.
  5. Add the rinsed rice to the pot and stir until the rice is well coated with the tomato mixture.
  6. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, ensuring that it covers the rice by about an inch. Bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked and has absorbed all the liquid.
  8. If using protein, you can add it during the last 10 minutes of cooking to allow it to heat through.
  9. Once cooked, fluff the rice with a fork and let it sit covered for an additional 5 minutes before serving.

Additional Information

Jollof rice is often served with fried plantains , coleslaw, or a side of salad. It's a versatile dish that can be customized with various proteins and vegetables based on personal preferences.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Jollof rice!
